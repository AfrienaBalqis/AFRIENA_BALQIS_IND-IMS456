About Me

My favourite flower - Tulip

My most favourite flower, would always be Tulip. The most known meaning of tulips is perfect and deep love. They're ideal to give to someone who you have a deep, unconditional love for, whether it's your partner, children, parents or siblings.

My favourite food - Nasi Lemak

Nasi lemak isn't just a dish for me; it's a comforting embrace of flavors and memories. Each bite is a symphony of textures and tastes that awaken a sense of nostalgia and contentment. Whether enjoyed during family gatherings or as a treat after a long day, nasi lemak reminds me of home and the simple pleasures that bring comfort and happiness.

My favourite book - Girl In Pieces

"Girl in Pieces" is my favorite book because it provides a raw and authentic portrayal of trauma, resilience, and the journey toward healing. Charlie Davis's story of overcoming immense pain and adversity resonates deeply with me, capturing the complexity of mental health struggles with compassion and honesty. The book's powerful narrative and emotional depth offer both comfort and inspiration, reminding me of the strength of the human spirit and the importance of hope and self-acceptance.

My favourite drama - A Good Bad Mother

"A Good Bad Mother" is my comfort drama because it beautifully portrays the complexities of parent-child relationships and the transformative power of love and forgiveness. The journey of Young-soon and Kang-ho, from a strained relationship to a heartwarming reconnection, resonates deeply with me. The drama's blend of emotional depth, touching moments, and themes of family bonds offer a sense of warmth and reassurance, making it a perfect escape and a source of comfort.
